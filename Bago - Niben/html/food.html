Delicacies in Bago City
"Rice Grananary of Negros Occidental"

Bago City for one is known as the "Rice Granary of Negros Occidental."
Local delicacy makers use their local rice and sugar to make kakanin like bitso-bitso, ibus, kwakoy, but-ong, and suman latik.
